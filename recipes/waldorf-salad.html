Waldorf Salad

What is Waldorf Salad?

A Waldorf salad is a fruit and nut salad that features a mayonnaise dressing.

It doesn't traditionally contain meat, but chicken is often added to make it a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 3 apples â€” peeled, cored, and chopped
  • 1 cup thinly sliced celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/2 cup raisins (Optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, and salt together in a serving bowl.
  3. Stir in apples, celery, walnuts, and raisins. Cover and chill in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
